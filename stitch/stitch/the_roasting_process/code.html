01. Sourcing & Origin

Green Bean Selection

Our journey begins in the high-altitude volcanic soils of Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra. We seek "Green Gold"—unroasted seeds with moisture content precisely between 10% and 12%.

Every lot is subjected to a rigorous physical evaluation: density checks, size grading, and defect identification. We only select beans that exhibit the structural integrity to withstand our high-intensity roasting curves.
