The Carbon Ritual

01

Solar Roasting

Our micro-roastery in Vermont is powered by a 50kW on-site solar array, offsetting 100% of our energy consumption during peak production.

02

Circular Water Use

We utilize closed-loop filtration for our processing stations, saving over 450,000 gallons of water annually compared to traditional washing.

03

Local Soil Support

100% of our coffee chaff and expired grounds are donated to local organic compost facilities to enrich community soil.
