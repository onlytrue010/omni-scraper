01. Introduction

"Your privacy is not just a policy; it's a promise. We believe transparency is the foundation of the artisanal community we are building together."

Welcome to Estate & Bean. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make a purchase from our site. We are committed to ensuring that your experience remains as pure as our single-origin roasts.

By accessing our services, you acknowledge the practices and policies outlined in this documentation. We reserve the right to modify this policy as our craft evolves.

02. Data Collection

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device.

person_search

Device Information

Log files that track actions occurring on the site, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, and date/time stamps.

shopping_cart

Order Information

When you make a purchase, we collect your name, billing address, shipping address, and payment information.

Additionally, we collect "Order Information" including your name, billing address, shipping address, payment information, email address, and phone number. We refer to this automatically-collected information as "Device Information."

03. How We Use It

We use the Order Information that we collect generally to fulfill any orders placed through the Site (including processing your payment information, arranging for shipping, and providing you with invoices and/or order confirmations).

  • check_circle Communicate with you regarding your bean roast preferences and order status.
  • check_circle Screen our orders for potential risk or fraud to protect our artisanal ecosystem.
  • check_circle Provide you with information or advertising relating to our new origins or brewing techniques.

04. Security Measures

Encrypted at the Source

We use industry-standard encryption protocols to ensure that your transactional data remains confidential. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers; all payments are processed through certified, high-security gateways.
